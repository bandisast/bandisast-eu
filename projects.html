Sappho-PDA

Sappho-PDA serves as the driver for the Toshiba TCD1103GFG PDA on the Beaglebone Black microcomputer. This driver was used in my thesis (Scattering measurements with the Beaglebone microcomputer for portable biomedical sensors). It is written in C and PRU assembly, but the repo also contains a few extra goodies.
